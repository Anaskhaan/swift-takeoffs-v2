Your Constructed Question Answers

Find answers to all your construction estimating queries in one place. Our comprehensive FAQ section addresses your concerns with clarity and detail.

What Do Your Material Takeoffs Include?

Our material takeoffs provide a comprehensive breakdown of all necessary materials for your project. This includes detailed quantities, specifications, and descriptions for items such as concrete, lumber, steel, drywall, and other essential materials. We ensure that every aspect is covered to help you accurately plan and budget your project.

What Do You Need In Order To Do A Takeoff Or Estimate?

To provide an accurate estimate, we typically require your project blueprints (plans), specifications, and any specific trade instructions you may have. Digital formats like PDF are preferred.

Do You Charge For Reviewing Plans Before Placing An Order?

No, we offer a free initial plan review to understand the scope of your project and provide a detailed quote for our estimating services.

What Is Your Average Turnaround Time?

Our standard turnaround time is 24 to 48 hours for most projects. Larger or more complex projects may take slightly longer, which we will specify in your quote.

Do You Offer Pre-Bid And Post-Bid Meetings To Discuss The Projects?

Yes, we value collaboration. We are available for meetings to discuss the details of our takeoffs and help you prepare for your final bid submission.
